About

Welcome to my portfolio! I am Ama, a passionate and dedicated graphic designer from Ghana. With a strong educational background and a Masters degree, I have honed my skills in various design tools and techniques. My journey in graphic design started in 2020, in the final year of my Bachelor's degree and since then, I have developed a keen eye for detail and a love for creativity.
